## Brain Dump Session Template

When feeling overwhelmed, follow this:

1. **CAPTURE (5 min)** - Write everything. No filter.
2. **BREATHE (1 min)** - It's all out. You're safe.
3. **CATEGORIZE (3 min)** - Label each item
4. **ROUTE (5 min)** - Send to proper homes
5. **DELETE (1 min)** - Remove vents and noise
6. **PRIORITIZE (2 min)** - What's the ONE thing?
